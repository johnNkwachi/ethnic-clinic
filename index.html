Improve Your Body and Mind

Care at Ethnic Integrative Medical Clinic is built on integrative and natural medicine. We believe the body is a unique composition that responds to nature’s elements and evidence-based natural therapies – supporting healing, balance, and long-term wellness.

Naturopathy

Naturopathy combines modern treatment methods with traditional natural therapies. It is an alternative form of medicine that supports the body’s self-healing. We focus on the root cause of illness and treat accordingly, using acupuncture, herbal medicine, nutrition, and lifestyle guidance to promote long-term health and wellbeing.

Phytotherapy

Phytotherapy uses evidence-based plant medicines to support your health. We use carefully selected herbs and botanical preparations to address a wide range of conditions, always tailored to your individual needs and in line with modern clinical evidence.

Acupuncture & Acupressure

Acupuncture is based on traditional medicine that works with the flow of energy through the body’s meridians. Fine needles or acupressure are used at specific points to restore balance, relieve pain, and support wellness. Our practitioners are trained in both traditional and modern approaches.

Homeopathy

Homeopathy offers individualized treatment using highly diluted natural substances to stimulate the body’s healing response. We take into account your full health picture – physical and emotional – to select the most appropriate remedies for lasting improvement.

Chinese Medicine

Traditional Chinese medicine assesses and treats the whole person. We use herbal formulas, acupuncture, and lifestyle advice to restore balance and support your body’s natural ability to heal. Treatment is tailored to your constitution and current health needs.
